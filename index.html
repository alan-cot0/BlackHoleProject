About GalaxAI

GalaxAI was founded by two-time Hack(h)er413 contestants, Hope Roundcount and Alan Khoultchaev. Our goal is to reduce the need of expensive lab equipment and computing power to determine whether or not a spectroscopic reading indicates a dead galaxy or a black hole. We are using an AI algorithm and machine learning to quickly parse data and determine whether or not the user discovered a galaxy or a black hole. This will allow astronomers to unlock all secrets of the known universe at a fraction of the cost!
